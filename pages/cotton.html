About Cotton

Type of Soil: Cotton grows best in deep, well-drained soils with a pH of 5.8 to 8.0. Sandy loam soils are ideal.

Duration: The growth duration of cotton varies from 150 to 180 days depending on the variety and growing conditions.

Fertilizers Used: Cotton requires a balanced supply of nutrients. Common fertilizers include nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. Organic matter like compost can also be beneficial.
